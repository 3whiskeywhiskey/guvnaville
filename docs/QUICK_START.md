# Guvnaville Quick Start Guide

Get up and running in 5 minutes! This guide covers the absolute essentials to start playing Guvnaville.

## Installation & First Launch

1. **Download** Guvnaville from the official repository
2. **Extract** the archive to your preferred location
3. **Run** the executable for your platform:
   - Windows: `Guvnaville.exe`
   - macOS: `Guvnaville.app`
   - Linux: `Guvnaville.x86_64`
4. **Start** a new game from the main menu

## Your First 10 Turns

Follow this simple guide to learn the basics:

### Turn 1-2: Learn the Interface

**What you see:**
- **Top bar**: Your resources (Food, Water, Materials, etc.)
- **Map**: The game world with your starting units
- **Bottom right**: Minimap showing the full map
- **Bottom**: End Turn button

**Controls:**
- **Left Click**: Select units or tiles
- **WASD or Arrow Keys**: Move camera
- **Mouse Wheel**: Zoom in/out
- **Space or Enter**: End turn

### Turn 3-5: Gather Resources

**Goal**: Collect resources to survive

1. **Click on a unit** to select it
2. **Click on a nearby ruin** to move there
3. **Click the Scavenge button** (or press S)
4. **Gain resources** - each location has different resources:
   - Residential ruins ‚Üí Food, Medicine
   - Industrial ruins ‚Üí Materials, Fuel
   - Commercial ruins ‚Üí Electronics, Weapons

**Tip**: Always maintain food and water - your population needs them every turn!

### Turn 6-8: Build Your First Unit

**Goal**: Expand your faction

1. **Click on your base building** (usually center of your territory)
2. **Click the Production button**
3. **Choose a unit to build**:
   - **Scavenger**: Best for gathering (recommended first choice)
   - **Soldier**: Strong in combat
   - **Builder**: Can construct buildings
4. **Wait 2-3 turns** for production to complete

**Tip**: Building a second scavenger helps you gather resources faster!

### Turn 9-10: Explore and Expand

**Goal**: Discover the map and find more resources

1. **Select your units** (Tab key cycles through them)
2. **Move to unexplored areas** to reveal the map
3. **Look for resource-rich locations**:
   - Marked with icons on the map
   - Show resource type when you hover over them
4. **Avoid enemy units** (shown in red) for now

**Tip**: Press F1 anytime for in-game help!

## Your First Goals

Complete these objectives in your first game:

- [ ] **Survive 10 turns** without running out of food/water
- [ ] **Scavenge 5 locations** to gather resources
- [ ] **Build 2 additional units** to expand your faction
- [ ] **Explore 50% of the map** to find opportunities
- [ ] **Construct your first building** (housing or farm recommended)
- [ ] **Win your first combat** against an enemy unit (optional)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Not saving regularly**: Press F5 to quick save often!
2. **Ignoring food/water**: Always keep 3+ turns of supply
3. **Overextending units**: Don't move too far from base early on
4. **Attacking too early**: Build up strength before fighting
5. **Hoarding resources**: Use them to grow your faction!

## When Things Go Wrong

### "I'm out of food!"
- Immediately send all units to scavenge residential areas
- Look for buildings marked with food icons
- Build a farm ASAP for sustainable food

### "An enemy attacked me!"
- Move your units to defend your base
- Use terrain (high ground, fortifications) for defense bonuses
- Retreat weaker units to safety
- Consider loading your last save (F9) if battle goes poorly

### "I don't know what to do next"
- Press **F1** for the in-game help system
- Check the **notification panel** (right side) for alerts
- Follow the **tutorial** if you skipped it
- Read the **USER_MANUAL.md** for detailed information

## Next Steps

Once you're comfortable with the basics:

1. **Learn the Culture System**: Press C to open the culture tree
   - Earn points by playing
   - Spend points on powerful upgrades
   - Choose a specialization path

2. **Build Your Economy**: Construct buildings for production
   - Farm: Produces food
   - Workshop: Produces materials
   - Clinic: Produces medicine

3. **Prepare for Combat**: Build soldiers and fortifications
   - Check enemy locations on minimap
   - Build defensive structures
   - Equip units with weapons

4. **Engage with Events**: Make choices when events occur
   - Events appear as pop-ups
   - Your choices have consequences
   - Some events grant big rewards

5. **Pursue Victory**: Choose your path to winning
   - Domination: Destroy all enemies
   - Economic: Accumulate most resources
   - Cultural: Complete the culture tree
   - Survival: Last until turn 150
