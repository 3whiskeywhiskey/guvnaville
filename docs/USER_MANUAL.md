## Introduction

Welcome to **Guvnaville**, a post-apocalyptic turn-based strategy game set in the ruins of a once-great city. As the leader of a faction struggling to survive, you must scavenge resources, build your base, manage your population, and compete with rival factions for dominance.

### What Makes Guvnaville Unique?

- **Emergent Culture System**: Your faction develops unique characteristics based on your actions
- **Dynamic Events**: Random encounters and challenges keep every playthrough different
- **Resource Scarcity**: Careful management is key to survival
- **Tactical Combat**: Position and terrain matter in every battle
- **AI Personalities**: Each AI opponent has distinct behavior patterns

## Getting Started

### System Requirements

**Minimum:**
- OS: Windows 10, macOS 10.15, or Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+)
- Processor: Dual-core 2.0 GHz
- Memory: 2 GB RAM
- Graphics: OpenGL 3.3 compatible
- Storage: 500 MB available space

**Recommended:**
- OS: Windows 11, macOS 12+, or Linux (Ubuntu 22.04+)
- Processor: Quad-core 2.5 GHz
- Memory: 4 GB RAM
- Graphics: OpenGL 4.0 compatible
- Storage: 1 GB available space

### Installation

1. Download Guvnaville from the official repository
2. Extract the archive to your desired location
3. Run the executable:
   - **Windows**: `Guvnaville.exe`
   - **macOS**: `Guvnaville.app`
   - **Linux**: `Guvnaville.x86_64`

### First Launch

On your first launch:

1. The main menu will appear
2. Click **New Game** to start
3. The tutorial will automatically begin (recommended)
4. Follow the tutorial instructions to learn the basics

**Note:** You can skip the tutorial and replay it later from the main menu.

## Game Overview

### The Setting

Guvnaville was once a thriving metropolis, but after "The Collapse," it became a battleground for survival. Different factions emerged from the ruins, each competing for control of the remaining resources.

### Your Role

You are the leader (the "Guvna") of one such faction. Your decisions will determine whether your people thrive or perish in the harsh post-apocalyptic world.

### Game Flow

1. **Your Turn**: Move units, scavenge, build, and make decisions
2. **AI Turns**: Opponents take their actions
3. **End of Round**: Resources consumed/produced, events may occur
4. **New Turn**: The cycle continues

---

## Core Mechanics

### Turn-Based Gameplay

- Each turn represents one day in Guvnaville
- You have unlimited time to plan your actions
- Click **End Turn** when ready to proceed
- AI factions take their turns in sequence
- Resources are consumed and produced at turn end

### Action Points

- Units have limited movement per turn
- Actions like scavenging or building consume the unit's turn
- Units regain full action points at the start of your turn

### Fog of War

- You can only see areas within your units' vision range
- Unexplored areas are hidden
- Enemy units are only visible when in your vision range
- Use scouts to reveal the map

### Territory Control

- Capture locations by moving units to them
- Controlled locations provide benefits
- Defend your territory from enemy incursions

## Units and Combat

### Unit Categories

#### **Scavenger** üîç
- **Role**: Resource gathering specialist
- **Strengths**: Fast movement, finds more resources
- **Weaknesses**: Weak in combat
- **Best for**: Exploration and scavenging

#### **Soldier** ‚öîÔ∏è
- **Role**: Combat specialist
- **Strengths**: High attack and defense
- **Weaknesses**: Slow, expensive to maintain
- **Best for**: Fighting and defending

#### **Builder** üë∑
- **Role**: Construction specialist
- **Strengths**: Builds structures, repairs buildings
- **Weaknesses**: Very weak in combat
- **Best for**: Expansion and maintenance

#### **Trader** üí∞
- **Role**: Diplomatic specialist
- **Strengths**: Better trade deals, avoids conflict
- **Weaknesses**: Weak in combat
- **Best for**: Economy and diplomacy

#### **Scout** üî≠
- **Role**: Reconnaissance specialist
- **Strengths**: Very fast, increased vision range
- **Weaknesses**: Weakest in combat
- **Best for**: Exploration and intelligence

### Unit Statistics

- **Health**: Current hitpoints (max varies by unit type)
- **Attack**: Damage dealt in combat
- **Defense**: Damage reduction
- **Movement**: Tiles unit can move per turn
- **Vision**: How far unit can see

### Combat System

#### Initiating Combat

1. Move your unit adjacent to enemy unit
2. Select your unit
3. Click "Attack" action
4. Combat resolves automatically

#### Combat Resolution

Combat damage is calculated based on:

- **Attacker's Attack** vs **Defender's Defense**
- **Unit Health** (damaged units less effective)
- **Terrain Bonuses** (high ground, fortifications)
- **Equipment** (weapons, armor)
- **Experience** (veterans fight better)
- **Random Factor** (¬±10% variance)

#### Combat Outcomes

- **Victory**: Defender retreats or is destroyed
- **Defeat**: Your unit retreats or is destroyed
- **Stalemate**: Both units take damage, no victor

#### Terrain Bonuses

- **High Ground**: +20% attack
- **Fortifications**: +30% defense
- **Cover**: +15% defense
- **Open Ground**: No bonus

### Combat Tips

- **Numbers Matter**: Avoid fighting when outnumbered
- **Choose Battles**: Don't attack if chances are poor
- **Use Terrain**: Position on high ground and in cover
- **Heal Between Fights**: Damaged units are ineffective
- **Protect Weak Units**: Keep scavengers away from combat
- **Focus Fire**: Eliminate dangerous enemies first

## Culture System

### Overview

The Culture System represents your faction's development and specialization. Your actions earn Culture Points, which you spend on upgrades in the Culture Tree.

### Earning Culture Points

- **Scavenging locations**: 1-3 points
- **Winning battles**: 2-5 points (based on difficulty)
- **Constructing buildings**: 3-7 points (based on building type)
- **Completing events**: 1-10 points (based on outcome)
- **Trading successfully**: 1-2 points
- **Surviving turns**: 1 point per 5 turns

### Culture Tree Branches

#### **Military Branch** ‚öîÔ∏è

Focuses on combat effectiveness and warfare.

**Tier 1:**
- *Weapon Training*: +10% unit attack
- *Basic Tactics*: +10% unit defense
- *Rapid Mobilization*: -1 turn production time for soldiers

**Tier 2:**
- *Advanced Weapons*: Unlock advanced soldier types
- *Fortification Tactics*: +20% defense in fortifications
- *Aggressive Expansion*: Faster territory capture

**Tier 3:**
- *Elite Forces*: Unlock elite soldier unit
- *Total War*: All units gain +20% combat stats
- *Military Industry*: -50% cost for military units

#### **Economic Branch** üí∞

Focuses on resource gathering and trade.

**Tier 1:**
- *Efficient Scavenging*: +20% resources from scavenging
- *Basic Trading*: Better trade deals with AI
- *Resource Management*: -10% resource consumption

**Tier 2:**
- *Advanced Scavenging*: Can scavenge locations twice
- *Trade Routes*: Establish permanent trade routes
- *Industrial Production*: +50% production building output

**Tier 3:**
- *Master Trader*: Best possible trade deals
- *Resource Empire*: +100% all resource generation
- *Efficient Economy*: -25% all costs

#### **Technology Branch** üî¨

Focuses on advanced buildings and equipment.

**Tier 1:**
- *Basic Research*: Unlock research building
- *Tool Improvement*: +15% builder efficiency
- *Electronics Recycling*: Gain electronics from scavenging

**Tier 2:**
- *Advanced Buildings*: Unlock advanced building types
- *Power Generation*: Build generators (reduces fuel use)
- *Communication*: Better map intel, early event warnings

**Tier 3:**
- *Prototype Technology*: Unlock unique faction units
- *Automated Defense*: Automated turrets for defense
- *Research Victory*: Can win via technological superiority

#### **Survival Branch** üõ°Ô∏è

Focuses on population growth and resilience.

**Tier 1:**
- *Food Preservation*: Food lasts 50% longer
- *Water Purification*: +20% water from sources
- *Basic Medicine*: Faster unit healing

**Tier 2:**
- *Population Growth*: +50% population growth rate
- *Disaster Preparedness*: -50% damage from events
- *Improved Housing*: +50% population capacity

**Tier 3:**
- *Resilient Population*: Population never decreases
- *Medical Expertise*: Units heal to full each turn
- *Self-Sufficient*: Greatly reduced resource needs

### Strategy Considerations

**Specialization vs. Balance:**
- **Specialize**: Focus on one branch for powerful late-game bonuses
- **Balance**: Spread points for versatility and adapting to situations

**Early vs. Late Game:**
- **Early**: Economic and Survival help establish strong foundation
- **Mid**: Technology and Military prepare for conflict
- **Late**: Max out chosen branch for victory push

**Faction Personality:**
- Your culture choices define your faction's identity
- AI will respond differently based on your culture
- Some victory conditions favor certain culture paths

Natural and man-made disasters.

**Examples:**
- Toxic storm
- Building collapse
- Contaminated water source

Most events present multiple choices:

1. **Read Carefully**: Understand the situation

Some events trigger follow-up events:
- Your choices in early events affect later outcomes

Each AI faction has a distinct personality that affects their behavior:

#### **Aggressive** ‚ö°

- Expands territory rapidly
- Weak economy
- **Counter**: Strong defense, then counter-attack

#### **Defensive** üõ°Ô∏è

- Slow but steady expansion
- **Counter**: Attack early before they fortify

#### **Economic** üí∞

- Avoids combat when possible
- Strong in late game
- **Counter**: Disrupt economy, attack early

#### **Balanced** ‚öñÔ∏è

- Well-rounded approach
- **Counter**: Stay flexible, watch for patterns

#### **Diplomatic** ü§ù
- Seeks trade and alliances

## Victory Conditions

### Domination Victory
Eliminate all other factions by destroying their bases.

**Strategy:**
- Build strong military
- Expand aggressively
- Capture enemy territory
- Protect your base

### Economic Victory
Accumulate the highest total resource value by turn 100.

**Strategy:**
- Focus on economic culture branch
- Establish many resource sources
- Trade frequently
- Defend your economy

### Cultural Victory
Complete the entire culture tree in any branch.

**Strategy:**
- Earn culture points consistently
- Specialize in one branch
- Complete important events
- Survive long enough to max out

### Survival Victory
Be the last faction standing after turn 150.

**Strategy:**
- Focus on survival culture branch
- Maintain strong defense
- Avoid unnecessary conflicts
- Sustain your economy

## Tips and Strategies

### Beginner Tips

1. **Complete the Tutorial**: It covers all basics
2. **Start on Easy**: Learn mechanics without pressure
3. **Maintain Food/Water**: Always keep reserves
4. **Don't Overexpand**: Spread too thin = vulnerable
5. **Use Scouts**: Knowledge is power
6. **Save Often**: F5 is your friend
7. **Read Events Carefully**: Choices matter

### Intermediate Strategies

1. **Specialize Early**: Choose culture branch by turn 20
2. **Control Key Locations**: Hospitals, warehouses, etc.
3. **Build Defensively**: Protect critical infrastructure
4. **Trade Smart**: Don't give away valuable resources
5. **Scout AI Territory**: Know your enemies
6. **Maintain Unit Balance**: Mix of unit types
7. **Plan Production**: Think 5-10 turns ahead

### Advanced Tactics

1. **Bait and Ambush**: Lure enemies into traps
2. **Economic Warfare**: Raid enemy resources
3. **Diplomatic Manipulation**: Play factions against each other
4. **Rush Strategies**: Early aggression can pay off
5. **Turtle Strategy**: Fortify and outlast
6. **Culture Synergy**: Combine culture benefits
7. **Timing Attacks**: Strike when enemies are weakest

### Resource Management

**Food Chain:**
- Turn 1-10: Scavenge only
- Turn 10-30: Mix of scavenging and hunting
- Turn 30+: Farms for sustainability

**Economy Priorities:**
1. Food/Water (survival)
2. Materials (expansion)
3. Medicine (population health)
4. Weapons/Ammo (security)
5. Electronics/Components (advancement)

**Storage Strategy:**
- Keep 3-5 turns of food/water minimum
- Stockpile materials for big projects
- Trade excess for needed resources
- Don't hoard what you won't use

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Game Won't Start
**Solutions:**
- Check system requirements
- Update graphics drivers
- Run as administrator (Windows)
- Check game files integrity

#### Performance Issues
**Solutions:**
- Lower graphics settings
- Close background applications
- Update graphics drivers
- Reduce map size in new games

#### Save Game Not Loading
**Solutions:**
- Check save file isn't corrupted
- Ensure save file is in correct location
- Try loading from main menu instead of continuing
- Contact support if issue persists

#### Game Crashes
**Solutions:**
- Update to latest version
- Verify game files
- Check error logs
- Report bug with save file

### Bug Reporting

If you encounter a bug:

1. Note exact steps to reproduce
2. Save your game file
3. Take screenshots if relevant
4. Check logs folder for error logs
5. Report on GitHub issues page
6. Include system information
